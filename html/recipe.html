焼き芋

  • Preheat oven to 450 °F (230 °C)
  • Scrub under running water and dry well, this prevents it from steaming rather than properly baking
  • Poke all over with fork
  • bake for 30 minutes
  • Flip over for 30 more minutes (time depends on the size)
